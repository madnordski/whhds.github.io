The WHHDS Morning Ride
The Wingra Heron Hops and Derailleur Society proudly sponsors a one-hour ride every weekday morning starting at the Mills Street entrance of the Arboretum at 6:00AM. The ride goes out through the Arboretum, down Seminole to Whalen Road and back on Mutchler. Expect an easy warmup through the Arb, an 18 to 25mph paceline out and back (depending on how everyone is feeling that day), a zesty sprint finish up to the beltline, followed by a gentle cool down through the Arb. If this grabs you, send an email to Damon Bourne so we know to look out for you.

Our regular season, dictated by the sunrise, and to some extent temperature, runs from mid-April through mid-September. See the ride report for a detailed, no-holds-barred account of our season.

Because we're a serious cycling outfit, we have a few rules to keep out some of the riff-raff.

* It's true. 2000 will be the 17th year of morning rides!
