4/15/02 And so it begins...
The 19th season of the Wingra, Heron, Hops, and Derailleur Society began today at the Mills St. Entrance of the arboretum. A record crowd of 9 riders started the ride after a solemn, yet stirring opening ceremony led by the WHHDS directorship.

Some rode new bikes, some old, but all were bright eye and bushy tailed ready to start the 2002 campaign to renew friendships, watch the sun rise on a daily basis, and shed winter pounds accumulated through one too many beer, triple cream cheeses from France, and general slothfulness. Dear reader, yours truly is no less than -- I can't even get myself to say it-- let's just say, that I've put on a few this winter.
