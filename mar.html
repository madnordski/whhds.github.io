Madison Area Ride Groups

West Side

  • Wingra, Heron, Hops and Derailleur Society (WHHDS) - early morning weekday rides and Saturday rides
  • Women on Wheels - weekday evening rides led by women

East Side

  • Slow Roll Cycles - rides, usually at least partly off-road, leave at 6:20pm on Tuesdays. Weekly details can be found on Facebook.
  • Black Saddle - they seem to tend to ride on Thursday evenings, but they post rides on Instagram when they happen.
  • Machinery Row - they run a couple rides, including the Century Training Club, which ramps up to doing the Door County Century in September.
  • Motorless Motion - Taco ride leaves at 6pm on Wednesdays.
  • Brazen Dropouts - Saturday morning fast Row ride leaving from Machinery Row at 9am.
  • Madtown Mondays - party pace group ride leaving from Orton Park on Monday afternoons, though it may be worth confirming whether it is happening this year.
